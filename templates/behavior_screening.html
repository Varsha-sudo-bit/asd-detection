🧠 Behavioral Screening Questionnaire

1. Does the child maintain eye contact?
2. Does the child respond when called by name?
3. Does the child show repetitive movements?
4. Does the child prefer playing alone?
5. Does the child have delayed speech development?
6. Does the child avoid social interaction?
7. Does the child get upset with routine changes?
8. Does the child show unusual sensory behavior?
9. Does the child have difficulty understanding emotions?
10. Does the child avoid eye gaze frequently?
